Scholarvy FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scholarvy Spaces?

In short, it is a collaborative editor for students equipped with configurable and teacher-managed artificial intelligence.

What if students type from another screen to avoid detection of copy and paste?

The revision history of transcribed writings usually follows a straight and linear progression, unlike authentic writings, where students tend to review and rework their content in a more iterative back-and-forth manner.

Is Scholarvy an AI detector?

No. The Scholarvy ecosystem promotes the adoption of artificial intelligence in formative activities for their completion. In contrast to AI detectors, our approach is more proactive and constructive rather than restrictive.

Can students use human-like automatic writing simulators?

Tools and simulators for human-like automatic writing do not function in Scholarvy. Any attempt to bypass Scholarvy’s functions will be automatically blocked.
