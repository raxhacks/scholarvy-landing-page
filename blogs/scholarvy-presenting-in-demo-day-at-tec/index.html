Join us at our exhibition at the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day

We are excited to announce our participation at the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day.

Feb 18, 2024

See you at the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day!

We are excited to announce our participation at the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day. This event will take place next Tuesday and we will be presenting our project with enthusiasm and pride. We would love to have your presence and support during this important day.

What is the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day?

The Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day is a prominent event in the entrepreneurship community, where startups present their innovations to an audience made up of investors, industry experts, and other entrepreneurs. This event is a unique opportunity to showcase the work we have been doing and to connect with people who share our passion for innovation and technological development.

Our Exhibition: What to Expect?

During our exhibition, we will present our latest innovation, the Scholarvy Ecosystem. This ecosystem combines artificial intelligence with educational management to create a personalized and efficient learning experience. We will show how our solution can transform education, with the integration of artificial intelligence into the development process of training activities.

Why Should You Attend?

Attending the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day is an excellent opportunity to:

  • Learn about Innovations: Discover the ideas and technologies that are shaping the future.

  • Connect with Entrepreneurs: Network with other innovators and industry leaders.

  • Support the Local Ecosystem: Show your support for local startups that are working to make a difference in our community.

Acknowledgements

We want to take this opportunity to thank the Tecnológico de Monterrey for organizing this event and providing us with the platform to present our project. Their support is essential for the development of the entrepreneurship ecosystem in our region.

We look forward to seeing you!

Don't miss the opportunity to be part of this exciting event. Mark the date on your calendar and join us at the Emprendimiento Tec Demo Day. We are looking forward to sharing our work with you and hearing your valuable feedback.

We hope to see you there and share this amazing experience together. See you at Demo Day!
