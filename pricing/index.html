Frequently Asked Questions

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy is designed with your satisfaction in mind. If you're not entirely happy with Scholarvy within the first 30 days of your subscription, you can request a full refund. To initiate a refund, please contact our support team with your account details and the reason for your request. We aim to process all refunds within 5-7 business days. This policy applies to both monthly and annual plans, giving you peace of mind when subscribing to Scholarvy.

Do you offer discounts for large institutional and district clients?

Yes, we offer customized pricing plans for large institutions and districts to ensure that our solution meets their specific needs at the best value. Discounts are available based on the number of users, subscription length, and required features. Institutional and district clients can also benefit from dedicated support, custom integrations, and training sessions. For a personalized quote and to discuss your institution's needs, please contact our sales team at luis.chapa@scholarvy.com.

How does the referral program work?

Our referral program is designed to reward our loyal customers for spreading the word about Scholarvy. For every new customer you refer who signs up for a paid plan, you and the referred party both receive a 20% discount on your next billing cycle. There's no limit to how many people you can refer, so the savings can really add up. To participate, simply share your unique referral link found in your account dashboard. Discounts will be automatically applied to your account once a referral's subscription is confirmed.
